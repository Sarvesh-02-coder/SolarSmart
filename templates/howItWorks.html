Understand the inner workings of our intelligent solar energy predictor

Step 1: User Input

You enter your location pincode, solar panel technology, area, and optionally tilt and manufacturer details on the homepage form.

Step 2: Live Weather Fetching

We query NASA POWER and OpenStreetMap APIs to fetch live solar irradiance, temperature, and wind speed for your location.

Step 3: ML Model Prediction

The model uses a Random Forest Regressor trained on global datasets to estimate your solar energy production using GHI, weather, and time features.

Step 4: Performance Adjustment

Adjustments are made using a performance factor based on your chosen panel material and manufacturer for improved accuracy.

Step 5: Visualization

You'll receive your current output, daily average, monthly and yearly generation, along with money saved, and two animated charts for last month and 10-day forecast.
